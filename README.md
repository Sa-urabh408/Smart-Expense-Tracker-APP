# Smart Expense Tracker

"Tired of doing complex math just to split a dinner bill? Meet **Smart Expense Tracker**—the ultimate financial companion for friends, roommates, and travelers! From snapping receipt photos on the go to magically calculating exactly who owes whom, this app takes the awkwardness out of shared costs. Dive into a seamless experience with smart debt settlements, personalized budgets, and premium analytics. Stop chasing debts and start making memories—manage your money smarter, together!"

## 🚀 Key Features:

*   👥 **Group Expense Management:** Create groups for trips, apartments, or events. Add members easily and start logging shared expenses instantly.
*   🧾 **Smart Splitting:** Split bills equally, by exact amounts, or by percentages. The app handles all the complex math in the background.
*   📸 **Photo & Receipt Attachments:** Snap and attach photos of your receipts directly to the expense.
*   💸 **Simplified Settlements:** Our intelligent algorithm calculates the most efficient way to settle up.
*   📊 **Detailed Transaction History:** Keep complete transparency with a clear history of 'who paid what' and 'who owes whom'. 
*   ✨ **Premium Experience:** Unlock advanced tracking, aesthetic UI features, and exclusive perks through our in-app premium subscription.
